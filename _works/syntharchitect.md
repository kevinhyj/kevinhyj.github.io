SynthArchitect is an agentic system for automated plasmid engineering. It integrates Golden Gate, DNA Cauldron, and other bioinformatics tools so design steps can become more composable, inspectable, and easier to connect with laboratory workflows.

This project reflects a broader direction I care about: scientific agents that do not replace experimental judgment, but make the slow parts of research more navigable.
